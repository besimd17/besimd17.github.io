The Artist

Besim Dauti

Painter · Pyrographer · Photographer · Illustrator

Besim Dauti is a multidisciplinary visual artist whose work spans the tactile warmth of woodburning to the layered depth of oil painting, the precision of drawing, and the captured moments of photography. Each medium is a different language through which he explores texture, light, and emotion.

His woodburning works — documented on the @bswoodcraft YouTube channel — fuse pyrographic tradition with contemporary imagery. His paintings and drawings carry a quiet intensity, while his photography distills fleeting beauty into lasting frames.
